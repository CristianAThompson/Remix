Steps

Open up Chrome on the remix.

If you are on the page on the remix then you can click the link below:

https://goo.gl/hiyV3i
If you aren't, type in the url above into the URL bar in Chrome

Once the page loads in either case click download

After it has successfully downloaded click the start menu icon on the Remix(The squigly d and e symbol)

Right Click on the Megabox HD Icon and select uninstall, follow the prompts to uninstall

After it has finished click the start menu again and open Downloads

Inside Downloads click on the item that says Megabox HD v1.0.3 (Ad Free).apk

Follow the prompts to complete install

After it finishes installing open the application by finding and clicking MegaboxHD icon

Find a video and try to play it, it will say you need to download a new video player. Click ok and it should open the google play store to Mplayer

Click Install and follow the prompt, after it finishes close all the open windows and Open MegaboxHD again

Find and try to play a video, it should as for permissions say yes then it should work as expected and it will open an ad inside the video player. To close the ad and watch the movie just move the mouse to the upper left of the windows and click the back button once

If it has an X in the upper left of the window(not on the white bar that drops down that will close MegaboxHD) you can click that to close the ad also
